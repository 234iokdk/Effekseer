Render - Ring

Overview

Drawing and parameters when "Ring" is selected in drawing setting are explained.

When "Ring" is selected, a circular effect is drawn.

The ring consists of a triple structure of outside, center and inside.

Parameter

Rendering Order

Specify from which sprite to draw in order from among sprites originating from the same node. In the case of generation order, the first generated is the last generated, the reverse of the generation order, the first generated is drawn before.

Configuration

Specify how to place sprites on particles. In the case of "Billboard", the sprite always points to the viewpoint side. In the case of "Z axis rotating building board", peel the viewing side while fixing the Z axis rotation. In the case of "Y axis fixed", peel the viewing side while fixing the rotation of the Y axis. When "fixed", the sprite follows the rotation of the particle.

Vertex Count

The number of corners of the figure to display. The more corners are, the closer it is to a circle, but you can dare to reduce it to a polygon.

Viewing Angle

The default is 360 degrees with a circle. You can reduce this value to a semicircle, fan shape.

Outer

It is the shape of the outer part of the circle. The value of x specifies the radius. The value of y specifies the height.

Inner

It is the shape of the inner part of the circle. The value of x specifies the radius. The value of y specifies the height.

Center Ratio

It is the ratio of the position of the center of the circle. If you want to center the inner part, specify 0.0. Specify 1.0 when placing the center part on the outside. To center the center, specify 0.5.

Outer Color

It is the color of the outer part of the circle.

Center Color

It is the color of the center part of the circle.

Inner Color

It is the color of the part inside the circle.
