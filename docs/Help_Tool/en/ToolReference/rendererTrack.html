Render - Track

Overview

Drawing and parameters when "Track" is selected in drawing setting are explained.

When "Track" is selected, effects on continuously connected loci will be drawn.

The track is drawn by connecting all the children of a certain particle instead of drawing each particle. For example, if there are particles 1, 2, and 3 of the child of particle 0, a shape is drawn with a track drawn between 1, 2, and 3. The track is shaped like connecting the lines between each particle. Unlike the ribbon, the ribbon was influenced by the rotation and enlargement of the particle, but in the case of the track it is not influenced by them and always faces the camera.

Parameter

Front Size

Specify the width of the front of the track.

Middle Size

Specify the width of the middle of the track.

Back Size

Specify the width behind the track.

Color, Left

Specify the color of the left front and left rear of the track.

Color, Left-Center

Specify the color of the left middle of the track.

Color, Center

Specify the color in front of the middle of the track and the rear in the center.

Color, Center-Middle

Specify the middle middle color of the locus.

Color, Right

Specify the color to the front right and back right of the track.

Color, Right-Center

Specify the color of the right middle of the track.
